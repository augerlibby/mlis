Goal 5: Effectively communicate and collaborate to deliver, market, and advocate for library and information services.

Outcome 5a. Apply marketing and/or advocacy principles to demonstrate and promote the value of libraries and information agencies.

LIS 707: Leadership, Marketing, and Strategic Communication

Marketing Plan: Bellwood Public Library

I created a marketing plan for a public library for the marketing plan assignment in LIS 707: Leadership, Marketing, and Strategic Communication. This assignment required us to suggest measurable objectives, audiences, key messages, and supporting statements for the key messages. Each objective encompasses strategies and tactics, and activities to accomplish the objective. This assignment applied knowledge gained from the course reading, Library Marketing and Communication: Strategies to Increase Relevance and Results by Cordelia Anderson, focusing on Chapter 3 of the text. I applied what I learned from this reading and other relevant course readings to this assignment, ultimately aiming to demonstrate and promote the value of this institution and its offerings.

Outcome 5b. Demonstrate effective professional communication to achieve common understanding as an individual or in group settings.

LIS 701: Core Values, Ethics, and Issues in Library and Information Profession

Competences & Standards Graphic & Presentation: Association for Library Service to Children

LIS 701: Core Values, Ethics, and Issues in Library and Information Profession’s Competences & Standards Graphic & Presentation assignment required students to work alongside a peer to complete the project. I collaborated with a fellow student, and we effectively communicated expectations and divided our responsibilities evenly so that we both contributed significantly to the assignment. I had a personal issue arise before the due date which complicated the process, but my classmate was understanding and ultimately we were able to complete the project in time and still fairly split the work. I found this group project to be effective, as both of us were willing to contribute and to communicate promptly and honestly about any issues that arose.

Outcome 5c. Demonstrate leadership principles in an educational, professional, or community context.

LIS 707: Leadership, Marketing, and Strategic Communication

Leadership Paper: Daring Leadership in Librarianship

The objective of the leadership paper assignment in LIS 707: Leadership, Marketing, and Strategic Communication was “to analyze the theories of leadership experts and current trends in information institutions and apply them to real life cases”. For this assignment, we were required to select a book on leadership, and I chose “Dare to Lead: Brave Work. Tough Conversations. Whole Hearts.” by Brené Brown. I sought out other scholarly sources on leadership and management to include in my paper. Then, I interviewed two librarians from the same institution. I applied the principles and theories I learned from the selected book and other sources and analyzed the conversations I had with the librarians. This allowed me to analyze leadership principles and apply them to a professional context.
